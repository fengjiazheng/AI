Model 1

Considering that significant improvements in natural language understanding (NLU) tasks (including NLI) come from using pre-trained Transformer-based models, we decided to use BERT as the base model for our work.

A base-less basic (110M parameter) BERT architecture was used as a starting checkpoint and fine-tuned based on the generated training data.

Source documents and declarative statements are used as inputs to the model, and bi-directional classification (consistent\inconsistent) is performed using a single-layer classifier based on [CLS] markup.

We refer to this model as the Fact Consistency Checking Model (FactCC).
